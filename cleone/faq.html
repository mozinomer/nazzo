Covid-19 Update

We are still shipping as normal, but in light of the spread of COVID-19 we’re taking all necessary precautions to keep our customers and colleagues safe.

Social distancing is in force in our fulfilment centre. All staff wear gloves and other hygiene equipment and wash their hands hourly. Due to COVID precautions and anticipated delays with our delivery partners, we are experiencing longer ship times. Currently, plant orders will ship within 2-5 business days after being placed. Once your IS shipped it may take an additional 3–5 days to arrive at your door.

We really appreciate your patience and understanding. If you have any questions, please get in touch with our customer service team using the help button below. Thank you, as ever, for your support.
